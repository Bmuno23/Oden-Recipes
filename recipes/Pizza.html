Ingredients you will need:

  • 2 1/2 cups of flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon fast rise yeast
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Toppings:

  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups pepperoni slices
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup shredded monterey jack cheese
  • 3 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, salt, sugar, and yeast.

  2. Once mixed, add a cup of water and a teaspoon of oil.

  3. Turn dough onto a floured surface and knead for 2 minutes.

  4. Place dough in a greased bowl, turn the grease to top.

  5. Cover and let rise for at least 20 min.

  6. Punch down then place on 12 in, greased pizza pan.

  7. Pat into a circle.

  8. Toppings: Mix first 5 ingredients and spread over crust.

  9. Spread sauce on dough then place pepperonis on top

  10. Sprinkle with cheese blend

  11. Add any extra peperoni on top

  12. Bake at 400* for 20 min or until light brown.

  13. Enjoy!
