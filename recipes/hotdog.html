Hotdog

Back to main page

Description:

Another widely eaten dish with multiple combinations. Originally coming from Germany(shocker) this unique recipe of sausage and bun can be professionally cooked or simply thrown into a microwave.

Its a staple food in todays American culture being served at only the most American of celebrations including the 4th of july. What can I say we Americans love our weiners ;).

I'm not sure why I have this on here as a recipe. Honestly if you can't figure this one out on your own(probs emily) then you shouldn't be cooking in the first place.

Anyways enjoy the recipe. I'll be giving you two versions one easy for when you're in a rush and another for when you have too much time on your hands.

Ingredients you will need:

  • Hot dogs
  • Hot dog Buns

Steps:

  1. On a paper plate place 2 hot dogs and throw them in the microwave for 1 minute.

  2. Pull the out of the microwave and place them into two hot dog buns.

  3. Cover with condiments of your choice.

  4. Voila a delicious hot dog in less than 5 minutes.

More complicated method:

  1. Place two hot dogs onto an open flame grill for 2 to 5 minutes.

  2. Refer to previous method for rest of steps.
